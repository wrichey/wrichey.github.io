Patient Specific Breast Modeling

Recently, we measured breast deformation in the supine position using our image guidance system. We evaluated how well rigid registration compensated for these breast deformations. See a sneak peek below, and stay tuned for more info on this project.

54 Breast Deformation from Supine Imaging to Surgery within an Image Guided Approach (Richey) from Vise Vanderbilt on Vimeo.

For more relevant modeling, check out nonrigid modeling using the Linearized Iterative Boundary Reconstruction method, established by Heiselman et al. Recently, this method was expanded to model pressure changes during image guided liver surgery.
